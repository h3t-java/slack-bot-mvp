Company FAQ

How do I request vacation?
Vacation requests must be submitted through the HR portal. Your manager must approve the request before the vacation can be scheduled.

How many vacation days do employees receive?
Employees receive 20 days of paid vacation each year.

Can I work remotely?
Yes. Employees can work remotely up to two days per week if approved by their manager.

How do I contact IT support?
IT support can be reached through the IT Service Portal or by emailing support@company.com.
