IT Support Guide

Laptop Requests
Employees can request a new laptop through the IT Service Portal. Requests are usually approved within two business days.

Password Reset
If you forget your password, use the company password reset tool available on the login page. If the tool does not work, contact the IT helpdesk.

Software Installation
Employees may request new software through the IT Service Portal. All software requests must be approved by the employee's department manager.

Security Policy
Employees must not install unauthorized software on company devices. All laptops must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems remotely.
